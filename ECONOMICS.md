# Economics

## What a converted lead is worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. Based on the
publicly available information about the market, a typical deal
is a startup switching $500-2000/month of AI spend to Credex
credits at a 20-30% discount.

If Credex takes a 15% margin on credits resold, a customer spending
$1000/month is worth $150/month in gross profit, or $1,800/year.
Assuming 12 month average retention, LTV per converted customer
≈ $1,800.

Conservative estimate: $800 LTV per converted customer.
Optimistic estimate: $2,500 LTV per converted customer.
Working assumption: **$1,200 LTV**.

## CAC at each channel

| Channel | Est. visitors | Conversion to audit | Conversion to lead | Conversion to customer | CAC |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Hacker News | 300 | 25% = 75 audits | 30% = 22 leads | 5% = 1.1 customers | ~$0 |
| Reddit | 200 | 20% = 40 audits | 25% = 10 leads | 5% = 0.5 customers | ~$0 |
| Twitter | 150 | 15% = 22 audits | 20% = 4 leads | 5% = 0.2 customers | ~$0 |
| Direct outreach | 50 DMs | 40% = 20 audits | 50% = 10 leads | 10% = 1 customer | ~$0 |

At $0 paid budget, CAC is effectively $0 in cash. The real cost is
time — roughly 2 hours/week of community engagement and outreach.
At a $50/hour opportunity cost that's $100/week or $400/month.
At 8 customers/month from organic, CAC ≈ $50 in time cost.

## Conversion funnel math

- Audit completed → email captured: 25-35%
- Email captured → consultation booked: 10-15% (only high-savings cases)
- Consultation booked → credit purchase: 30-40%
- Overall: audit completed → purchase ≈ 1-2%

At 1,000 audits/month: 10-20 purchases.
At $1,200 LTV: $12,000-24,000 monthly recurring gross profit.

## What would have to be true for $1M ARR in 18 months

$1M ARR = ~$83k/month revenue.
At 15% margin that requires ~$550k/month in credits sold.
At $1,200 average annual contract, that's ~460 active customers.

To get 460 customers in 18 months:
- Need roughly 25-30 new customers per month by month 12
- At 1-2% audit-to-purchase conversion, need 1,500-3,000 audits/month
- At 25% audit completion rate from landing page, need 6,000-12,000
  monthly visitors by month 12

That's achievable with:
- SEO picking up on comparison keywords by month 6
- 1-2 viral HN/Reddit posts
- Credex's existing customer base seeding early word of mouth

The math works if the tool gets to 10,000 monthly visitors within
a year. That's not guaranteed but it's a realistic target for a
genuinely useful free tool in this space.

**The thing that has to be true:** the audit has to be accurate
enough that users trust the recommendations. One bad recommendation
that costs someone money kills word of mouth. The audit logic needs
to be defensible — which is why every recommendation has a
one-sentence reason citing specific plan features and pricing.
