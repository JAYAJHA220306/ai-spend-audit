## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes, completely free. No credit card, no account required. You
get the full audit instantly — email is optional and only asked
after you've seen your results.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every price in the audit engine is sourced directly from the
vendor's official pricing page with a verification date. We update
it regularly. The sources are documented publicly in PRICING_DATA.md
in the GitHub repo.

**Q: What AI tools does it cover?**
A: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI
API, Gemini, and Windsurf. More tools will be added based on demand.

**Q: What does Credex do with my data?**
A: Your audit data is stored to generate your shareable link. If
your audit shows significant savings, Credex may reach out — but
only if you provide your email, and only to offer help, not to spam
you. The public shareable version of your audit strips out your
email and company name.

**Q: Why is Credex offering this for free?**
A: The audit helps you find overspend. For cases with large savings
opportunities, Credex can often help you get the same tools at lower
cost through discounted credits. The tool is genuinely useful on its
own — the Credex upsell only appears when it's actually relevant.
